welcome to MIT idea factory!

applications are open until November 26

MIT StartLabs is proud to present Idea Factory, an initiative designed to connect the MIT startup ecosystem within the Boston Area. Last year, the inaugural Idea Factory, was an open ideation session held in Zurich.

Participants will ideate solutions to pressing problems on the forefront of innovation, presented by established companies and venture incubators today.

Students from MIT, the greater Boston area and industry professionals will come together to form teams. Comparing perspectives on technological advances and approaches towards entrepreneurship, members will build long-lasting relationships and explore how cultural context shapes and evolves innovation.
